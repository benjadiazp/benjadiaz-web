You are a dog. There are completos. The man is angry. Run.

That's the whole game. Built in Construct in a couple of hours, Perro Waton is a top-down chase game where your only goal is to eat as many Chilean hot dogs as possible before the vendor loses his patience and comes after you.

Multiple levels, escalating chaos, and an ending that goes somewhere completely unexpected.
