You play as a bear. A stealthy one. Your mission: sneak through a secret laboratory full of patrolling robots and surveillance cameras without getting caught.

That's the whole pitch. Give it a try — it's free to play on itch.io.

<VideoPreview src="https://www.youtube.com/shorts/VrsjdOIacMo" title="Making Oso Sigiloso" />

## What to Expect

The robots patrol set paths and have a vision cone. Step into it and they'll chase you down. Surveillance cameras sweep the room and set off a full alert the moment they spot you. Get caught by either and the level resets. The goal is simply to reach the exit — undetected.

It's a short game, built as a prototype, but it's a complete one. There's a level to navigate, enemies that react to you, and a bear that walks (animated from scratch, joints and all).

## Behind the Game

Oso Sigiloso started as my undergraduate research project at Universidad del Bío-Bío. The research was about documenting the minimum steps to build a 3D game — and the game itself was the proof of concept. Every asset was made from scratch over a single weekend: 3D model, textures, animation, music, sound effects.

The full story of how it was made is in [the blog post](/blog/oso-sigiloso).
